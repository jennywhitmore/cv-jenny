Profile

With over 10 years of experience in graphic design, especially focusing on prototyping and mock-ups, I recently trained on UX/UI.
Along with it, comes my special interest in coding, alongside with my curiousity in finding out about the tech part behind the design.
But for me, in order to gather the courage and believe in my own abilities, and, at the age of 40, go for the developer part, it needed a role model woman already working in tech.
Thanks to my wife's encouragement and to my children, who definetly were the most overwhelming change in my life, I guess doing something that I always felt facinated by doesn't seems like such a big deal after all.
I am also not afraid of new challenges. As a curious person, I like to keep learning while managing the casual everyday routines. The combination of both feels pretty good.

I would like to use my skills to help develop code for everyone and work in an inclusive and barrier-free manner, without thinking in boxes.
